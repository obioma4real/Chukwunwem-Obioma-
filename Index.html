About Me

Chukwunwem Obioma — The Rising Voice Making Waves in Gospel Music

Chukwunwem Obioma is a fast-rising 15-year-old gospel minister and recording artist from Anambra State, Nigeria, currently based in Benin City. Young but deeply anointed, he is already making waves in the gospel industry with his passionate delivery, bold message, and unique sound.

A devoted Salvationist, Chukwunwem worships at The Salvation Army, where his parents serve as respected and well-known ministers of the gospel across Nigeria. Growing up in a strong ministry home shaped his spiritual foundation and ignited his desire to use music as a tool to preach Christ.

In 2025, he stepped fully into the scene with the release of his debut single “To See Jesus (Intimacy Anthem)”, followed by “Good God”, a joyful declaration of God’s faithfulness. His style blends worship, afro-gospel rhythms, poetry, and a youthful vibrance that sets him apart among his generation.

Driven by purpose, Chukwunwem’s mission is clear: to preach Jesus, inspire his generation, and shine the light of the gospel through every lyric and every sound.
